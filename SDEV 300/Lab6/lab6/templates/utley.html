Chase Utley

Chase Utley is my single most favorite player that I have ever seen. He had a remarkable way of playing the game, day in and day out. He was tough nosed and above all, was a damn good player. Below are some player highlights.

Chase's Career Highlights

  • 6x All-Star, 4x Silver Slugger
  • His WAR 7 (Peak 7 years) is 49.3 which ranks 9th among Second Basemen
  • Pivotal member of the 2008 Championship winning Philadelphia Phillies
